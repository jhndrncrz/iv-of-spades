We are
IV of Spades.

IV of Spades is a Filipino rock band from Metro Manila, Philippines, consisting of vocalist and rhythm guitarist Unique Salonga, vocalist, bassist and keyboardist Zild Benitez, vocalist and lead guitarist Blaster Silonga, and drummer Badjao de Castro.

Unique Salonga

Unique Torralba Salonga, known mononymously as Unique or stylized as UNIQUE, (born April 26, 2000) is a Filipino singer-songwriter and musician. He began his career as a vocalist and original frontman of IV of Spades and later pursued a solo career.
